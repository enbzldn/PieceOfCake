# Delete a Recipe

1. Open **Recipes** page by tapping **Go to recipes**.
2. Find the recipe you want to delete and swipe right to left.
3. Tap **Delete**.
4. Confirm the deletion by tapping **Delete** again, otherwise tap **Cancel**.
